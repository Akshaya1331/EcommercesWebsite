About AKKI

Our mission was (and still is) simple: we wanted to create Clothes that is both beautiful AND comfortable. Dress you can live-in, and love-in, and gets better with age. Designed in California and hand-made in Mexico and Brazil, all AKKI dress is crafted with time and care (each pair takes over 1 week to make) using only the softest vegetable-tanned cloth we can find. Add in a molded arch for extra comfort and you have what we think are the most timeless, feminine, luxurious and comfortable shoes in the market.
